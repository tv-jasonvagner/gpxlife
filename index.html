See it in action.

Built for the trail, not the cloud.

Every feature in GPX Life works without a network. Your routes live on your device and (if you opt in) your private iCloud — never on our servers, because we don't have any.

🗂️

Import any GPX

Drop-in from Mail, Files, Strava exports, AllTrails, Komoot, Garmin Connect — anything that emits standard GPX.

🗺️

Apple Maps & OSM

Native Apple Maps rendering with OpenStreetMap as a fallback. No tracking, no third-party tile servers.

↩️

Reverse & remix PRO

One tap to reverse a route into a separate library entry. Auto-detect U-turns and trail junctions as named waypoints. Keep the original untouched.

🌤️

Live weather

Open-Meteo forecasts at your start point — wind, precipitation, temperature — pulled fresh, never logged.

📤

Hand off anywhere

Export back to Strava, Komoot, Gaia, Caltopo, or any GPX-aware app via the iOS share sheet.

☁️

Your device, not our cloud

Your library lives on your device — no account, no login, no servers. Import straight from iCloud Drive and back up your whole library to it with one tap.

Privacy & Terms

GPX Life is built around a simple principle: your route data is yours. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking — nothing for us to leak because there's nothing for us to keep.

Read the full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for the details on where your data lives, the third-party services involved, and the terms of use.
