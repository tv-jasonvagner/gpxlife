Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026.

GPX Life is designed around a simple principle: your route data is yours, and we don't want a copy of it.

Data we collect

None. GPX Life has no backend server, no user accounts, and no analytics. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information, route data, location history, or device identifiers. There is nothing for us to leak because there is nothing for us to keep.

Where your data lives

  • On your device. Imported GPX files, derived versions, and app preferences are stored in the app's local sandbox. Your library is per-device and does not sync across devices.
  • In iCloud Drive (your choice). You can import GPX files from, and export a full JSON backup of your library to, iCloud Drive. Those files stay in your personal iCloud Drive where you put them — we never see or touch them.

Third-party services

  • Apple Maps & MapKit. Used for map rendering. Subject to Apple's privacy policy.
  • OpenStreetMap. Used as a fallback tile source. Map tile requests include only the tile coordinates and a generic user agent — never your route or identity.
  • Open-Meteo. Used for weather forecasts. When you view weather for a route, the start coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo's public forecast API. Open-Meteo states that no personal data is logged. See open-meteo.com/en/terms.
  • Apple StoreKit. In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We receive a yes/no entitlement; we never see your payment details.

Children's privacy

GPX Life is rated 4+ and contains no targeted advertising, no third-party tracking, and no user-generated social features. It is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted here with an updated "last updated" date. Material changes will also be surfaced in-app.
